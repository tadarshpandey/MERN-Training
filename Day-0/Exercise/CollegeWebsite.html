BBS College of Engineering and Technology (BBSCET)

Location: Shantipuram, Phaphamau, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh

Affiliation: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) & Approved by AICTE

About the Institution

Established in 2002 under the Beni Madhav Shiksha Samiti, BBSCET is spread across a 27-acre campus in Prayagraj. The college focuses on delivering high-quality technical education, combining robust theoretical learning with active student-led engineering environments.

Academic Programs

The college offers several undergraduate and postgraduate degrees:

Program Level Courses Offered
Undergraduate (B.Tech) Computer Science & Engineering, AI & ML, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communication (ECE)
Postgraduate Master of Computer Applications (MCA), Master of Business Administration (MBA), M.Tech
Other Streams BCA, BBA, B.Pharm, Law (LLB / BA LLB)

Campus & Student Culture

  • Tech Ecosystem: Active student communities, peer networks, and code development circles.
  • Major Events: Hosts competitive technical events including the Yukti Hackathons and Robot Rumble.
  • Facilities: Well-equipped computer labs, a central library with over 45,000 books, on-campus hostels, and dynamic sports grounds.
  • Placements: Supported by a dedicated Corporate Relations and Training Cell (CRTC) that coordinates on-campus recruitment and technical training.
